Personal

I was born and raised in Cork, Ireland. I currently live there while I complete my undergraduate degree in Astrophysics in University College Cork. I am keen to travel outside the country to pursue a higher degree once I graduate in September '14. When I'm not drowning in assignments and other work, my favourite hobbies (and biggest passions) are archery and scouting.

Archery

I have been a member of UCC Archery Club since September '13 and a squad member for over 9 month. I practise for hours at a time twice a week both with UCC and with Cork City Archery Club. I own a 70"/32# Cartel Fantom. My indoor record in 445/600, which is respectable for someone at my level.

Scouting

I have been involved in Scouting for almost 18 years. I started in Beavers at the age of 4 and progressed through Cubs, Scouts, Ventures, and Rovers. Now, I am a Cub and Venture leader with 4th Cork. I love working with young people and leading them on the kinds of adventures I had when I was a youth member. Last summer we took our Scouts and Ventures to the World Scout Centre in Kandersteg, Switzerland. Scouting is one of the most character-building and enabling environments for youths and adults alike. I owe much of who I am today to the experiences I have in Scouting.
